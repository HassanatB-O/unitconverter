Metric/Imperial unit Conversion

Length (Meter(s)/Feet)

0.000 meter(s) = 0.000 feet

0.000 feet = 0.000 meter(s)

Volume (Liters/Gallons)

0.000 liter(s) = 0.000 gallons

0.000 gallons = 0.000 liters

Mass (Kilograms/Pounds)

0.000 kilo(s) = 0.000 pound(s)

0.000 pound(s) = 0.000 kilo(s)

Distance (Meters per second/Kilometers per hour)

0.000m/s = 0.000km/h

0.000km/h = 0.000m/s
